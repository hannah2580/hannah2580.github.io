London Restaurant Viability Finder
May 2026 Visualisation →
Geospatial decision tool that scores every London borough on its suitability for opening a restaurant. Scores are based on a weighted combination of demand (population density + TfL foot traffic), affordability (income deprivation index), and competition (existing restaurant density), with all variables normalised to a 0–1 scale.
